## Your California Privacy Rights

Subject to legal exceptions, California residents have the right to:

- **Know/Access** the categories and specific pieces of personal information collected about you
- **Delete** personal information
- **Correct** inaccurate personal information
- **Opt out of the sale or sharing** of personal information
- **Limit** the use/disclosure of sensitive personal information (where applicable)
- **Non-discrimination** for exercising your rights

---

## How to Exercise Your Rights

Submit a request at **privacy@privue.ai**. We will take **reasonable steps to verify your identity**. You may **authorise an agent** to act on your behalf, subject to verification and proof of authorization.

---

## How We Determine Retention Periods

Where a specific period is not listed above, we determine retention using documented criteria such as:

1. Legal, regulatory, tax, and accounting requirements
2. Security, fraud-prevention, and incident-response needs
3. The nature, scope, and sensitivity of the data
4. Operational requirements to provide, maintain, and improve the services
